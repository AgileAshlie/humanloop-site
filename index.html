Human-Centered Health, By Design

For people who look “fine on paper or social presence” but feel depleted, overwhelmed, or stuck navigating modern workplaces, and healthcare systems.

The Problem People Feel

  • Care and Communication are fragmented across specialists
  • Symptoms and Support are managed in isolation
  • Stress and Burnout are normalized
  • People are left to connect the dots themselves

When systems are overloaded — human or organizational — performance rarely fails all at once; it erodes.

The Gap

No one helps translate medical advice into daily life. No one supports behavior change in real time. No one holds the whole human system together.

This is where capable people stall — not from lack of effort, but from lack of coordinated support.

About Me

Ashlie Stevens is a Duke University–trained Health & Wellness Coach who brings over two decades of experience in coaching, leadership development, and human systems navigation — informed by both professional practice and lived experience.

Her work reflects a client-centered, evidence-informed approach to behavior change, shaped by experience across complex personal and professional systems. This includes guiding individuals and teams through periods of transition, supporting the development of self-efficacy and autonomy, and navigating intergenerational and organizational dynamics.

Ashlie is known for deep listening, building durable trust, and partnering with clients to clarify goals, align actions with values, and adapt thoughtfully as conditions change. She brings a calm, grounded presence that supports sustainable change, resilience, and forward movement within real-world constraints.
